    NETSCOUT is pleased to host our customers, vendors, and employees at our annual ENGAGE event. With the health and safety of all attendees as a top priority, NETSCOUT will require all employees, customers, and vendors attending our private, invitation-only ENGAGE 2022 event to be fully vaccinated. No exemption requests will be entertained for customers or vendors attending this event. NETSCOUT will follow recommended Centers for Disease Control (CDC) guidelines for related safety protocols, so please check back for any additional updates as we get closer to the event.
